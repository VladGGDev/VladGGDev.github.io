About Me

Hello, my name is Vlad, I am years old and I'm a Romanian programmer and game developer. When I don't play video games, I spend my time working on various projects such as this very website. I have been coding since I was 12 years old and have gained a lot of experience. Currently, I mostly make games with C# in the Unity game engine or the MonoGame framework, but I can also code in C++, make websites, and use Python.

About This Website

This website was made for fun and is the place where I will post links to my games, post blogs, and upload web experiments. This could be also considered a portofolio website, being representative of my frontend skills and containing lisks to my other works.
